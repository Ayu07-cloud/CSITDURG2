Chhatrapati Shivaji Institute of Technology, Durg (CSIT) is a premier engineering and pharmacy institution in Chhattisgarh, established in 1999.

Affiliated to Chhattisgarh Swami Vivekanand Technical University (CSVTU), Bhilai and approved by AICTE, CSIT offers B.E., M.Tech, B.Pharma and D.Pharma programs.

The campus features smart & digital classrooms, advanced departmental labs, a well-stocked library, hostel, sports complex, cafeteria, gym, bank/ATM, medical centre, and a dedicated placement cell.

// 03 — PROGRAMS & ADMISSIONS

Academic Programs

B.E. / B.Tech

Undergraduate Engineering
  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with 45% (PCM)
  • Entrance: JEE Main / CG PET
  • University: CSVTU, Bhilai
  • Approval: AICTE
  • 8 Specializations Available
~₹ 75,000/ year

M.Tech

Postgraduate Engineering
  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech with 50%
  • Entrance: GATE Score
  • University: CSVTU, Bhilai
  • Approval: AICTE
  • CSE, ME, EE Specializations
~₹ 67,500/ year

B.Pharma

Bachelor of Pharmacy
  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 PCB/PCM with 50%
  • Approval: PCI (Pharmacy Council)
  • Career: Drug Dev, Clinical Research
  • Industries: Pharma, Healthcare, R&D
~₹ 60,000/ year

D.Pharma

Diploma in Pharmacy
  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB/PCM
  • Approval: PCI
  • Career: Pharmacist, Retail Pharmacy
  • Fast track into pharma sector
~₹ 40,000/ year
